Khao Soi

Description

From northern Thailand, a noodle soup with an amazing mix of flavor and texture.

Ingredients

For 6 servings

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 shallots, chopped
  • 3 gloves garlic, minced
  • 0.25 cups red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 4 cups coconut milk
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 0.5 cups vegetable oil
  • 12 whole dried Thai chili peppers
  • 6 small shallots, cut into quarters
  • 0.5 heads bok choy, chopped
  • 1 (8 ounce) package rice noodles
  • 1 cup pickeld mustard cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 0.5 cups coarsely chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
